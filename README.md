# Bike-Sales-Excel-Dashboard

Excel Dashboard analysing bike sales

[here the Excel sheet] [Excel Project Dataset (1).xlsx](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/29647660/Excel.Project.Dataset.1.xlsx)


## Objective

To identify key factors influencing bike purchase decisions across customer demographics and build an interactive Excel dashboard for visual exploration of consumer patterns.

## Dataset

- Records: 1,026 customer entries
- Variables: Age, Gender, Income, Marital Status, Education, Occupation, Commute Distance, Region, Number of Cars, Home Ownership, Bike Purchase (Yes/No)

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
  - Data Cleaning
  - Pivot Tables
  - Charts
  - Interactive Dashboard

## Analysis Covered

- Average income comparison between bike buyers and non-buyers by gender
- Purchase rate across age brackets (Adolescent, Middle Age, Old)
- Regional distribution of bike purchases (Europe, Pacific, North America)
- Impact of commute distance on purchase decision
- Occupation and education level segmentation

## Key Findings

- Higher income customers showed higher purchase rates
- Middle age bracket had the highest purchase volume
- Shorter commute distance correlated with higher bike purchase likelihood
- Professional and skilled manual workers showed distinct purchase patterns
